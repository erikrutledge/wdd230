Overview

Purpose

My purpose in creating this chamber of commerce website for Sun Valley, Idaho is to serve as a central location for residents or visitors to learn about the services of the city. This website will contain features to act as a community bulletin board, promote local businesses, and encourage healthy economic growth.

Scenarios

  • Are there any upcoming events being held by the commerce soon?

  • What local businesses are included in the commerce network?

  • Are there any job listings in the area?

  • As a visitor to Sun Valley, what things are there to do?
