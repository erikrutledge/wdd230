Sun Valley Chamber of Commerce

The Chamber has served the residents of Sun Valley since being founded in 1936 and has no plans on stopping! The chamber prides itself on the variety of ways it has helped the community such as promoting local economy growth, coordinating events, facilitating networking among local businesses, increasing opportunity for our residents, and many more.

While the main priority of the commerce is to help the residents of Sun Valley, we love and appreciate the year-round visitors who travel from far and wide to enjoy our lovely town. This hamber of commerce website serves as a great resource for visitors to see what Sun Valley has to offer. Before you plan your next visit, check the "Upcoming Events" board so you don't miss out on any incredible vacation memories. Also consider looking through the "Company Spotlight" to see which local businesses are going above and beyond to make Sun Valley the most unique community in the entire United States.
